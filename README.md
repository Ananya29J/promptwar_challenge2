# VoteWise

## ᓚ PROPOSED SOLUTION <br/>

VoteWise is a high-fidelity, interactive web application designed to transform complex electoral processes into an engaging, gamified, and personalized experience. Built with a focus on civic education and voter empowerment, it utilizes AI to provide tailored guidance for every citizen.
<br/>

## ᓚ Key Features<br/>

### ⟫ 👤 Digital Twin Profile:<br/>
An AI-driven wizard that analyzes your voter profile (age, awareness, location) to generate a personalized "Election Journey" checklist and dashboard.<br/>

### ⟫ 🎮 Interactive Simulator: <br/>
A gamified "Election Game" where users navigate real-world voting scenarios, learning the consequences of their choices in a safe, simulated environment.<br/>

### ⟫ 🔍 Know Your Candidates (Dual Mode)[All data is AI generated for now]:<br/>
Ongoing Mode: Explore candidate backgrounds, track records, assets, and campaign promises.<br/>
Completed Mode: View election results with an AI-highlighted winner, a summary of the losing candidates, and a "What They Are Doing Now" section for the elected representative.<br/>

### ⟫ 🔮 "What-If" Scenario Engine:<br/>
An AI-powered engine that analyzes hypothetical election situations (e.g., "What if I lose my voter ID?") and provides immediate, short-term, and legal consequences.<br/>

### ⟫ 🗺️ Interactive Flow Map:<br/>
A visual, step-by-step guide to the entire Indian election journey, from registration to the final announcement of results.<br/>

### ⟫ 🤖 AI Chat Assistant:<br/>
A persistent, context-aware chatbot trained on Indian electoral laws and procedures to provide instant answers to voter queries.<br/>
